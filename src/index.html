TURKEY

"Where East meets West: Explore the beauty and diversity of Turkey."

INFORMATION

CURRENCY

Currency: The official currency of Turkey is the Turkish lira (TRY). It's recommended to exchange money at official exchange offices or banks rather than street vendors.

TIMEZONE

Timezone: Turkey is in the Eastern European Time zone (EET), which is 2 hours ahead of Coordinated Universal Time (UTC+2) and observes daylight saving time.

LANGUAGE

Language: The official language of Turkey is Turkish, but many people in tourist areas and larger cities may speak English, especially those working in the tourism industry. It's always helpful to learn some basic phrases in Turkish to communicate with locals.

VISA

Visa requirements: Depending on your country of origin, you may need a visa to enter Turkey. Citizens of many countries can obtain an e-visa online before traveling. It's recommended to check the latest visa requirements before booking your trip.

TRANSPORTATION

Transportation: Turkey has a well-developed transportation network, including buses, trains, and domestic flights. Taxis and ride-sharing services are also widely available. In Istanbul, the metro and tram are convenient ways to get around the city. It's always a good idea to check transportation schedules and prices before traveling.
